Title: Infi-gen

Story: You're trapped in the infi-gen, an infinitely-generating dungeon, hence the name.
Maybe there's a way out? 

Game Mechanics:
* grid system of 32x32 squares
* turn-based movement and combat
* randomly generating walls, exits, enemies, and items

Player Mechanics:
* start with a base weapon 
* can only hold two weapons at a time
* can store up to 10 health pots in a bag
* can combine weapons

Enemy Mechanics:
* simple monsters, just hit you
* will try to take items

End Game: restart with no items except base weapon
